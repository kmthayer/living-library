Kelly M. Thayer Living Library

The Kelly M. Thayer Living Library is an interdisciplinary scholarly collection spanning computational molecular biophysics, chemistry, theology, literature, mathematics, history, music, pedagogy, and scientific computing.

The library is an ongoing and growing project intended to support teaching, research, interdisciplinary scholarship, and intellectual community.

The collection includes materials related to:

  • Computational Molecular Biophysics
  • Physical Chemistry
  • High Performance Computing
  • Artificial Intelligence and Scientific Computing
  • Theology and Biblical Studies
  • Literature and Poetry
  • History of Science
  • Mathematics and Physics
